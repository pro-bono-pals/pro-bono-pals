Meet ProBonoPals! The new probono service match maker. This application pairs entry level professionals who want to build their portfolio and give back to the community with local people who need probono services such as a small business owner or an elderly person in the neighborhood. This opprtunity allows the service provider to do good as they hone their craft and build their experinece in a low risk environment. The person recieving the probono service they need is enabled to recieve quick advancement on the project they want to complete or the portion of their life that they want to improve at no cost. This also provides a net working opportunity for all parties involved.
